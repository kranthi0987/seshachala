Seshachala-vanadarshani

This Seshachalavanadarshani website is step forward by the forest department A.P to provide a facility to book accommodation in places like Talakona and Mamandur. A responsible tourists to natural areas which is sustainable, equitable, community based endeavor for improving the living standards of the indigenous host communities and to create a stake in conservation of the environment besides offering the possible best to the nature loving tourists.

Talakona

Talakona waterfalls is the largest waterfalls in A.p, which is a paradise for natural lovers. This place consists of many waterfalls caves and natural habitat making it very interesting to visit. CBET Talakona provides many facilities which includes accommodation and can be booked in advance here. Talakona means 'head of hill' in Telugu. This place is believed to be the starting point of 'Seshachalam Hills' which are extended till Tirumala.
