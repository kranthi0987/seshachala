Jungle Book natural forest park

  • Junglebook, Eco tourism, waterfalls

Description :

Jungle book is developed by forest department, in Tirupati near kapilatertham it is a natural jungle made into a secured park to encourage people learn about forest habitat and benifits of direct contact with nature.

Jungle book has several attractions like Deer crawl, Insecrorium with wide variety of rare insects, Rock thrills for people who are intrested in trekking, Kids corner which is a kids play area, Boating in the Gundalacheruvu and waterfalls during rainy season in Malwadigundam.

Reaching this place i easy beacause its present in Tirupati itself and people who visit here can also visit KpilaTertham which is very famous temple present under the sacred Tirumala hills.
